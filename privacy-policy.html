Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Serotonin ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use the Serotonin mobile application (the "App").

Privacy First: Your data belongs to you. All mood tracking, task data, and personal information is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data to third parties.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide Directly

  • Profile Information: Your first name and last name during onboarding
  • Mood Data: Your mood check-ins, intensity levels, and optional notes
  • Task Information: Tasks you create, their categories, completion status, and associated mood data
  • Recording Data: Audio recordings you create using the Meeting Recorder feature (stored locally on your device)

2. Information from Google Services (Optional)

If you choose to connect your Google account, we access:

  • Email Address: To identify mentions of you in Google Workspace documents
  • Google Tasks: To import and sync your tasks
  • Google Calendar: To find meetings you attended with attached notes
  • Google Drive Files: To search for meeting notes and documents (Docs, Sheets, Slides)
  • Google Docs Content: To extract action items from meeting notes

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not store your data on our servers (all data is local)

How We Use Your Information

Local Data Storage

All personal information (mood data, tasks, profile) is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. This data never leaves your device except when:

  • You explicitly sync with Google services
  • You use AI features that require external processing (transcription, action item extraction)

Google Integration

When you connect your Google account, we use your data to:

  • Import tasks from Google Tasks into the App
  • Search your calendar for meetings with attached notes
  • Extract action items from Google Docs that mention your name
  • Scan Google Workspace documents (Docs, Sheets, Slides) for mentions of your email address
  • Display document context and provide links to open documents

AI Processing

When you use AI-powered features (Meeting Recorder, Action Item Extraction):

  • Audio Transcription: Audio recordings are sent to OpenAI's API for transcription using their gpt-4o-transcribe model
  • Action Item Extraction: Meeting transcripts and document content are processed by AI to identify tasks and action items
  • These services are subject to their respective privacy policies (OpenAI Privacy Policy)

How We Share Your Information

Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google APIs: For Google Workspace integration (OAuth, Tasks, Calendar, Drive, Docs, Sheets, Slides)
  • OpenAI API: For audio transcription and AI-powered action item extraction

These services process data according to their own privacy policies. We only send the minimum necessary data to these services to provide the requested functionality.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Google API Services User Data Policy

Serotonin's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Limited Use Disclosure

Serotonin's use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the following:

  • We only request access to Google user data that is necessary to provide and improve our user-facing features
  • We do not transfer Google user data to third parties except as necessary to provide or improve user-facing features, comply with applicable law, or as part of a merger or acquisition with user consent
  • We do not use or transfer Google user data for serving ads, including retargeting, personalized, or interest-based advertising
  • We do not use or transfer Google user data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes

Scopes We Request

When you connect your Google account, we request the following scopes:

  • userinfo.email - To identify mentions of you in documents
  • tasks - To import and sync your Google Tasks
  • calendar.readonly - To find meetings with attached notes
  • drive.readonly - To search for meeting notes and documents
  • documents.readonly - To read Google Docs content for action items
  • spreadsheets.readonly - To search Google Sheets for mentions
  • presentations.readonly - To search Google Slides for mentions

All access is read-only except for Google Tasks. We never modify your Google Drive files, Calendar events, or other documents.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Local Encryption: All data stored on your device is encrypted
  • Secure Authentication: Google OAuth 2.0 with automatic token refresh
  • HTTPS/TLS: All network communications use encryption
  • Minimal Data Transfer: We only send data to external services when necessary for specific features

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • View Your Data: All your data is accessible within the App
  • Delete Your Data: You can clear mood history, tasks, or disconnect Google at any time in Settings
  • Export Your Data: Contact us to request a copy of your data

Google Integration

  • You can disconnect your Google account at any time in Settings
  • Disconnecting will revoke the App's access to your Google data
  • You can manage permissions in your Google Account settings

Opt-Out

  • All Google integration features are optional
  • You can use the core mood tracking and task management features without connecting Google
  • You can disable auto-sync at any time

Children's Privacy

Serotonin is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date above. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@vibecode.app
  • App: Serotonin by Vibecode

Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable data protection laws including GDPR (for European users) and CCPA (for California residents). If you are a resident of the EU or California, you have additional rights under these laws, including the right to request deletion, access, and portability of your data.
